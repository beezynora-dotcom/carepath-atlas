Reimagining Teen Health Education

CarePath Atlas helps teens understand symptoms through clinical reasoning — not diagnoses — while contributing to anonymized public health research.

Get Involved

Why This Matters

Many teens experience health symptoms without understanding how medical professionals evaluate them. This leads to anxiety, misinformation, and delayed care.

What Makes This Different

  • Educational, not diagnostic
  • Built specifically for teens
  • Ethically designed and transparent
  • Open-source and research-oriented
