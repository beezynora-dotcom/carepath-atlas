Our Mission

CarePath Atlas is a student-led initiative focused on improving teen health literacy by teaching clinical reasoning — how doctors think, not what to self-diagnose.

Ethical Design

This project does not provide medical diagnoses. All educational content is reviewed for safety, and any collected data is anonymized and optional.

Collaboration

We welcome students, educators, developers, and health professionals who want to contribute to ethical health education and research.
